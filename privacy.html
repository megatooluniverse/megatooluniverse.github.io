Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At MegaToolUniverse, your privacy is our priority. We operate predominantly as a client-side web application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal data like names or passwords on our servers. Your saved tools, pinned tools, and recently used tools are stored locally in your browser using "LocalStorage".

2. Use of Cookies and Ads

We use third-party advertising networks, including Monetag, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

3. Third-Party Links

Our tools may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those third-party sites.

4. Data Security

Since we do not store user data on our servers, there is no risk of a data breach from our end. However, please ensure your own device is secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: megatooluniverse.dev@gmail.com.
